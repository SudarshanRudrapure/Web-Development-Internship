## Creating a PHP script
A PHP script is executed on the server, and the plain HTML result is sent back to the client. 
A PHP script starts with <?php and ends with ?>. 

## Basic php Syntax

<?php
//codd
?>


Case sensitivity
In PHP, NO keywords (e.g. if, else, while, echo, etc.),
classes, functions, and userdefined functions are casesensitive. 
However, all variable names are casesensitive.


<?php
ECHO"Hello World!<br>";         //output:Hello World
echo"Hello World!<br>";         //output:Hello World
ECHo"Hello World!<br>";         //output:Hello World
